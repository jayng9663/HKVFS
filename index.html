Welcome to 424th HKVFS

We are a small group who play Falcon BMS from Hong Kong, and our target is to let more hongkongers know about BMS.

We mostly focus on Mulit-role, doing both AA and AG. Sometime we would also do training for formation flight, Air to Air Refuel and a bit of Aerobatics to increase teamwork ability. <
